Dues & Donations

Support Us!

Dues

In order for us to provide all the awesome services that we do, (race reduction costs, transportation, uniforms, team dinners, and weekly runs) we need your support! Members of the team are REQUIRED to pay dues.

Here is how to pay your dues

  1. Follow this link.
  2. Fill out the appropriate fields.
    1. Under “Purpose of Payment” select “Student Organization”. Then select “GW Club Cross Country Team” from the dropdown menu.
    2. Under “Payment Information” select “Dues” from the “Payment Purchase” dropdown menu.

Returning members and new members alike pay $25 a semester (you get a free team tee!). If you are racing, dues are $50 a semester, you can either pay all at once or by the semester.

What is SUPER IMPORTANT is that you send your confirmation e-mail to gwuclubxc@gmail.com so that WE KNOW YOU HAVE PAID.

If you are in chrome and the link isn’t working try an incognito window.

Donations

Any donations are greatly appreciated and would be to pay for races, new uniforms, and travel expenses!

Here is how to make a donation

  1. Follow this link.
  2. Next, fill out the appropriate fields.
    1. Under “Purpose of Payment” select “Student Organization”. Then select “GW Club Cross Country Team” from the dropdown menu.
    2. Under “Payment Information” select “Other” from the Payment Purchase dropdown menu and specify Donation.

We appreciate your donation! Thank you!
